Successful Presentations
Through Storytelling

After being approached by a number of audience members from the business world, Mike began to run training sessions for those who wanted to improve their communication skills in the workplace. As the number of requests grew, Mike founded the company Be-Heard, with fellow storyteller Tim Ralphs. Be Heard offers expert training in all aspects of presentation skills, with a special focus on utilising the power of narrative to get one's message across clearly.

Mike’s storytelling sessions for the Day of the Dead family event at the Wellcome Collection were the perfect way to celebrate Mexico's most beloved tradition.
