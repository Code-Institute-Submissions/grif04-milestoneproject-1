About Mike


Mike has been telling tall tales all his life and decided to turn this into a career in 2008. Drawing his tales from Britainâ€™s rich heritage and from the four corners of the earth, His delivery is warm and engaging with a penchant for the strange and unexplainable.

A secondary school teacher for 19years and working within Mexico between 2004 - 2008, his students taught him many strange and magical Mexican tales, Mike discovered by using the artform of Storytelling in classes, it became a powerful teaching tool and has incorporated these stories into his performances.

Mike founded the company BeHeard with fellow storyteller Tim Ralphs. Be-Heard offers expert training in all aspects of your presentation skills, with a special focus on utilising the power of narrative to get one's message across clearly.
