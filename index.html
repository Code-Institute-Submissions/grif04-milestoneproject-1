About Mike


Mike has been telling tall tales all his life and decided to turn this into a career in 2008. Drawing his tales from Britainâ€™s rich heritage and from the four corners of the earth, His delivery is warm and engaging with a penchant for the strange and unexplainable.

A secondary school teacher for 19years and working within Mexico between 2004 - 2008, his students taught him many strange and magical Mexican tales, Mike discovered by using the artform of Storytelling in classes, it became a powerful teaching tool and has incorporated these stories into his performances.

About BeHeard

After being approached by a number of audience members from the business world, Mike began to run training sessions for those who wanted to improve their communication skills in the workplace. As the number of requests grew, Mike founded the company BeHeard, with fellow storyteller Tim Ralphs. Be Heard offers expert training in all aspects of presentation skills, with a special focus on utilising the power of narrative to get one's message across clearly.

The Power Of Narrative

Be Heard was created to help professionals deliver their message clearly, concisely and memorably. We will help our clients to focus on their audience and their message, utilising a range of exciting, practical storytelling techniques to shape their ideas into a powerful, coherent narrative.
